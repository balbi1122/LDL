Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our loan products and services

General Questions

What is Lite Doc Loans?

Lite Doc Loans is a hard money lender specializing in real estate financing. We offer quick approval, minimal documentation requirements, and flexible terms for various types of real estate loans.

How quickly can I get approved?

We can approve your loan in as little as 5 days, compared to traditional lenders who may take 30-60 days. Our streamlined process focuses on the property's value and your experience rather than extensive documentation.

Application Process

What documents do I need to apply?

We require minimal documentation compared to traditional lenders. Typically, you'll need:

  • Property information and purchase agreement
  • Renovation plan and budget (for fix and flip loans)
  • Contractor information (if applicable)
  • Basic personal information

How do I start the application process?

You can start by filling out our online application form or contacting our team directly. We'll guide you through the process and help you gather the necessary information.

Loan Types

What types of loans do you offer?

We offer several types of real estate loans:

  • Construction Loans
  • Fix and Flip Loans
  • Bridge Loans
  • DSCR Loans

What are the typical loan terms?

Loan terms vary by type:

  • Construction Loans: 6-24 months
  • Fix and Flip Loans: 6-12 months
  • Bridge Loans: 3-12 months
  • DSCR Loans: 5-30 years

Eligibility

What are the basic eligibility requirements?

Our eligibility requirements focus on the property and your experience rather than personal credit. Basic requirements include:

  • Property value documentation
  • Clear exit strategy
  • Minimum equity in the project
  • Experience in real estate (for certain loan types)

Do you require a minimum credit score?

We focus on the property's value and your experience rather than credit scores. While we may check credit, it's not the primary factor in our lending decisions.
